## Introduction

Connecting to various data warehouses is a fundamental feature of Multiwoven, enabling teams to streamline their data integration processes.

<img
  className="block"
  src="https://res.cloudinary.com/dspflukeu/image/upload/v1707531725/Multiwoven/Docs/Sources_mpzdhn.png"

This overview provides a general guide on how to connect your data warehouse sources, such as Snowflake, Redshift, BigQuery, and Databricks, to Multiwoven.

## Understanding the Connection Process

The process of adding a data warehouse source to AI Squared involves a few key steps. This overview aims to give you a clear understanding of the sequence and requirements for each step, ensuring a smooth setup. Specific details for each type of data warehouse will be provided in their respective dedicated sections.

### Step 1: Navigate to the Sources Section

To begin, you'll need to access the AI Squared dashboard. From there:

1. Locate the `Setup` section on the sidebar.
2. Click on the `Sources` section to proceed.

### Step 2: Add a New Source

Within the Sources section:

1. Find and click on the `Add Source` button.
2. You'll be directed to a page where you can select the type of data warehouse you wish to connect.

<img
  className="block"
  src="https://res.cloudinary.com/dspflukeu/image/upload/v1708064326/Multiwoven/Docs/sources/image_qwedtc.png"
  alt="Hero Light"
/>

### Step 3: Select Your Data Warehouse

1. Choose the data warehouse you're connecting to from the provided list. This selection determines the credentials you'll need to enter in the next step.

<img
  className="block"
  src="https://res.cloudinary.com/dspflukeu/image/upload/v1708064567/Multiwoven/Docs/sources/image_2_zxcjik.png"
  alt="Hero Light"
/>

### Step 4: Enter Required Credentials

Based on your selection:

1. Fill in the necessary credentials specific to your chosen data warehouse. This typically includes information like user IDs, passwords, API keys, or connection strings.

<img
  className="block"
  src="https://res.cloudinary.com/dspflukeu/image/upload/v1708064326/Multiwoven/Docs/sources/image_1_hh4uas.png"
  alt="Hero Light"
/>

### Step 5: Test the Connection

Before finalizing:

1. Test the connection to ensure that all details are correct and that AI Squared can successfully connect to your data warehouse.

<img
  className="block"
  src="https://res.cloudinary.com/dspflukeu/image/upload/v1708064654/Multiwoven/Docs/sources/image_3_qokeiw.png"
  alt="Hero Light"
/>

### Step 6: Name and Describe Your Source

Finally:

1. Assign a name and a brief description to your connected source. This helps in identifying and managing your sources within Multiwoven.

<img
  className="block"
  src="https://res.cloudinary.com/dspflukeu/image/upload/v1708064326/Multiwoven/Docs/sources/image_4_qow7vf.png"
  alt="Hero Light"
/>


<Tip>
For detailed instructions specific to your data warehouse, please refer to the dedicated section for each Data warehouse within our documentation.
</Tip>

Once these steps are completed, your data warehouse will be connected to Multiwoven, enabling you to move data seamlessly to various SaaS destinations.
